# Automate Your Treasury Operations: Introducing the GTreasury Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the GTreasury integration! Developed by Velocity BPA, this node brings enterprise treasury management automation to the n8n ecosystem.

## The Challenge of Treasury Management

Enterprise treasury teams juggle complex workflows involving cash positioning, payment processing, foreign exchange transactions, and risk management. These processes often require manual data transfers between GTreasury and other business systems—a time-consuming and error-prone approach that can delay critical financial decisions.

For organizations using GTreasury as their treasury management system, the lack of seamless automation between GTreasury and other enterprise applications has been a persistent pain point. Financial teams need real-time data synchronization, automated payment workflows, and integrated reporting across their entire tech stack.

## Introducing the n8n-nodes-gtreasury Integration

Our new GTreasury node bridges this gap, enabling n8n users to build powerful automation workflows that connect GTreasury with hundreds of other applications. Whether you're synchronizing cash positions with your ERP, automating payment approvals, or creating custom FX transaction workflows, this node provides the flexibility you need.

## Key Features

The GTreasury node for n8n empowers treasury teams to:

- **Automate cash positioning**: Automatically pull cash position data and distribute it to stakeholders or business intelligence tools
- **Streamline payment workflows**: Create automated payment processing pipelines that integrate with approval systems and accounting platforms
- **Manage FX transactions**: Build workflows that monitor exchange rates and execute FX transactions based on custom business rules
- **Integrate with your stack**: Connect GTreasury seamlessly with ERPs, accounting software, messaging platforms, and analytics tools

## Getting Started

Installing the GTreasury node is simple. In your n8n instance, run:


npm install n8n-nodes-gtreasury


After installation, restart n8n, and you'll find the GTreasury node available in your node palette.
